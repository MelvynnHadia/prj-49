Paper Camels



Some interesting facts about camels are:

  • Camels have furry ears.
  • Camels can easily move around sand because of their unique feet
  • Camels will try to drink as much water as they can if they find a water spot

Paper Pandas



Some interesting facts about pandas are:

  • Giant pandas are good at climbing trees and can also swim.
  • Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
  • An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day!

Paper Bears



Some interesting facts about bears are:

  • Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals.
  • Bears grieve deeply for others.
  • Bears have excellent senses of smell, sight and hearing.
